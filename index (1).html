DIRECTOR

Our Director is a highly accomplished and dedicated mentor with an inspiring journey in the field of defence education. He is a two-time CDS qualifier, securing impressive All India Ranks of 515 and 621, reflecting his strong academic excellence and deep understanding of competitive exams. With over 15 years of teaching experience, he has guided and mentored hundreds of students who have successfully cleared prestigious examinations like NDA, CDS, and AFCAT (written). An expert in English, his teaching style is known for clarity, discipline, and motivation, helping students build both confidence and competence. Having completed his own schooling from Navodaya Vidyalaya, he carries forward the same values of dedication, hard work, and excellence. His vast experience, result-oriented approach, and passion for teaching continue to inspire students to achieve their goals and serve the nation with pride.
